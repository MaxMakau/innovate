Welcome to Innovate and Elevate Academy

our mission

To equip a generation of innovative and transformative Africa's youth with personalised mentorship, SDG-focused training, start-up build-up, and suitable job placement.

Our vision

We envision a vibrant, prosperous Africa where young leaders drive impactful change towards economic growth, social development, and environmental sustainability.

We offer comprehensive training aligned with all 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), ensuring our graduates are ready to take on leadership roles that create lasting change.

Transforming Education, Empowering Futures

Cutting-Edge Curriculum

Our programs are designed to tackle the gap between formal education and the practical demands of the modern workforce, while fostering a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and sustainability.

Expert Mentorship

At Innovate and Elevate Leadership Academy, we are leading the way in addressing youth unemployment and sustainable development change in Africa.

Startup Ecosystem

Our academy is dedicated to empowering young people by providing a well-rounded education that combines mentorship, upskilling in technical and soft skills, job placement, and startup support.
